Lakhni : My Small Town

Economy & Agriculture

Lakhani's economy is predominantly agrarian. The region is known for cultivating Bhandara Chinnor rice, a non-Basmati aromatic variety that received a Geographical Indication (GI) tag in 2023.

Education

Late Nirdhan Patil Waghaye Arts, Commerce & Science College: Located on National Highway 6 in Gadegaon, this college offers undergraduate courses in arts, commerce, and science . Samarth Mahavidyalaya: Situated in Samarth Nagar, this institution provides higher education opportunities to the local population .

Natural Attractions

Nearby, the Koka Wildlife Sanctuary offers a haven for wildlife enthusiasts. Established in 2013 and covering an area of 92.34 square kilometers, the sanctuary is home to diverse fauna, including tigers, leopards, sloth bears, and migratory birds from Siberia during the winter season .
